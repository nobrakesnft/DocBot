## Getting Started

Welcome to SampleToken! Here's how to get started:

1. Visit our website at app.sampletoken.io
2. Connect your wallet (we support MetaMask, WalletConnect, and Coinbase Wallet)
3. Make sure you have some ETH for gas fees

## Staking

### How to Stake

1. Go to the Staking page
2. Enter the amount of SAMPLE tokens you want to stake
3. Click "Stake" and confirm the transaction
4. Start earning rewards immediately!

### Staking Rewards

- Current APY: 12%
- Rewards are distributed every block
- You can claim rewards anytime
- No minimum stake amount

### Unstaking

To unstake your tokens:

1. Go to the Staking page
2. Click "Unstake"
3. Enter the amount to unstake
4. Wait for the 7-day unbonding period
5. Claim your tokens

## Tokenomics

- Circulating Supply: 45,000,000 SAMPLE
- Staking Rewards Pool: 20% of total supply
